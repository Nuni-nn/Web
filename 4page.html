Scene 4

He tilts his head slightly, as if making a decision.
— “Would you like me to show you the castle? There are places here that most guests never see.”

Agree:

Heroine: “I would love that. This place must be full of untold stories.”

Hero (with a gentle smile): “Then trust me.”

Refuse:

Heroine: “Thank you, but I’d rather stay in the hall. I don’t want to leave the guests behind.”

Hero (calmly, with a trace of disappointment): “I understand. Then I won’t insist.”
