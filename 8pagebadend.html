BAD END

The silence is heavier than words. You pull your hand away, your heart twisting even as you make the choice.

His eyes widen — confusion and quiet pain reflecting in the moonlight. “[Name]…” he whispers, but you are already stepping back.

You vanish into the thinning crowd, leaving him on the balcony beneath the fading moon.

Later, you will remember this night — the music, the lights, the way his eyes searched yours. You will remember… and wonder what might have been.

But it will remain unfinished — a story without an ending.
